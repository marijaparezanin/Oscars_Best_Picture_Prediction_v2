Oscars Best Picture Predictor

        Pick 3 films you would like to nominate for The Best Picture award. Afterwords click the button to see the predicted winner! The winner is calculated on a multi-linear regression model that has been trained with the nominees and winners of the past 20 years in the category. The model takes into account many factors such as: IMDB score, Genre, RottenTomatos score, Audience score, Golden globe wins, runtime etc.
You can also select 3 films out of our 60+ film database from the past 2 decades and see which one would win against the chosen competitors.
